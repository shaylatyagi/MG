Our Vision

A world where every fleet operator and gig worker in India has the infrastructure, visibility, and tools to build a financially secure livelihood — on their own terms.

Our Mission

To close the three structural gaps — Time, Payment, and Asset — that hold India's gig workforce back, by building an operating ecosystem designed entirely around how they actually earn and live.

Why We Exist

Most people see the convenience.

Every day, millions of drivers, riders, and logistics workers power the Indian city — delivering food, ferrying passengers, moving goods. They are the circulatory system of the modern urban economy.

Yet the infrastructure built around them was never truly designed for how they live and work. Fleet operators — often small business owners managing 5 to 500 vehicles — carry enormous operational weight on fragmented, manual systems: WhatsApp messages, paper ledgers, and disconnected spreadsheets.

We spent months listening to the ground truth. What we found is not a technology problem. It is a design problem. The products built for this workforce were designed around the platform's convenience, not the person doing the work.

MobilityGrid is our answer to that.

The Problem

Three gaps that define the challenge

Through months of direct research, we identified three structural mismatches that compound into a system that consistently fails both the operator and the driver.

01
Time Gap
Daily earners in a monthly financial system
A driver earns every day. But traditional financial models — collections, credit, settlements — are built around monthly behaviour. This mismatch creates constant friction, missed collections, and an inability to plan with any certainty on either side.
02
Payment Gap
Fluid income, rigid collection systems
A driver deals in variable, fluid cash flows. Yet rent collection, deposits, and dues are handled through rigid automated systems or paper records that force friction. The result: disputes, defaults, and a breakdown of trust between operator and driver.
03
Asset Gap
A vehicle is their livelihood, not their own
The vehicle is a driver's primary tool of income. Yet access, payments, assignments, and documentation remain fragmented across WhatsApp, paper agreements, and disconnected systems. One unexpected event can erase weeks of earnings with no safety net.

The Platform

One ecosystem. Three stakeholders.

MobilityGrid is not a single app — it is a coordinated operating system with purpose-built interfaces for every actor in the fleet economy.

Super Admin

Full platform oversight from one console

The Super Admin Console gives the MobilityGrid team complete visibility over every company, fleet owner, driver, and payment on the platform — with tools to onboard, approve, and monitor at scale.

  • Company onboarding and fleet operator management
  • Driver KYC review queue — approve Aadhaar, PAN, DL, Bank
  • Real-time platform GMV and collections dashboard
  • Cross-fleet driver and vehicle registry
  • Role-based access — admin, manager, read-only

How It Works

Up and running in four steps

From fleet onboarding to your first digital rent collection — the entire process is designed to be simple, fast, and reliable.

1

Admin onboards your company

The MobilityGrid team verifies and activates your fleet on the platform. Usually under 24 hours.

2

Add your vehicles and drivers

Set vehicle details, assign drivers, and set daily rent amounts directly from the owner dashboard.

3

Drivers complete KYC

Drivers upload documents through the PWA. Admin reviews and approves — unlocking vehicle assignment.

4

Collections run digitally

Drivers pay rent via UPI. You see every payment in real-time with zero manual reconciliation.
